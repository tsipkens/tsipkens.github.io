Timothy A. Sipkens

ABOUT

Tim is an aerosol scientist interested in optical and physical diagnostics, with a particular focus on data inversion and analysis. My work has ranged from applied analyses on the products of gas flares to face mask construction.

Tim recieved his PhD from the University of Waterloo in 2018, with a focus on time-resolved laser-induced incandescence (TiRe-LII), an optical nanoparticle diagnostic that employs a nanosecond pulsed laser to heat particles.

CODE

As part of many of my projects, I have code available for use by researchers. Please refer to my GitHub page for more details. Some sample projects are listed below.
